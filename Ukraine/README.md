## ğŸ“‹ **What is the 72-Hour Switch?**

The **72-Hour Switch** is a humanitarian armistice framework that creates a verified 72-hour pause in hostilities to enable:

1. **Child Repatriation** - Safe return of forcibly deported children to their families
1. **POW Exchanges** - Medical treatment and exchanges at neutral zones
1. **Infrastructure Repair** - Emergency fixes to prevent humanitarian catastrophe
1. **Humanitarian Aid** - Food, water, and medical supplies to civilians
1. **Nuclear Safety** - IAEA inspection of at-risk nuclear facilities

**No political negotiations. No territorial changes. Just saving lives.**
